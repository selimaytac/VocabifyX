# VocabifyX – Product Vision

## Why This App Exists

Most vocabulary apps are built around a single use case: learning a foreign language. VocabifyX is different. It is a **general-purpose vocabulary specialization tool** — designed for anyone who wants to master the terminology of a specific topic or domain, regardless of which language that topic is in.

A German speaker can study German biology terms in their native language. A software engineer can drill down on programming vocabulary. A traveller can memorise English greetings. A medical student can learn anatomy terms in Latin. The scope is completely unlimited — **any topic, any language, any goal**.

VocabifyX is **topic-first, not language-pair-first**. You pick *what you want to learn about*, not just which languages you speak. AI generates a curated keyword list for you in seconds, and proven flashcard + quiz loops make sure you retain everything you learn.

## Core Value Proposition

> "Tell us what you want to master. We'll give you the exact vocabulary you need — and make sure it sticks."

1. **AI-powered list creation** – No manual word hunting. Describe your goal and get a curated keyword list in seconds.
2. **Topic-first approach** – Learn vocabulary around a specific context: biology, software, travel, law, medicine, or anything else.
3. **Any language, any domain** – Study in your native language or a foreign one. Biology terms in German, tech vocabulary in English, or daily phrases in any language you choose.
4. **Retention-first learning** – Flashcards, quizzes, and spaced repetition ensure long-term memory.
5. **Progress you can feel** – XP, levels, streaks, and achievements make every session rewarding.

## Strategic Goal

**Hypothesis:** Users will pay for a focused, AI-powered vocabulary tool that adapts to their specific learning goal — whether that goal is a foreign language, a professional domain, or anything in between — especially when the experience is gamified and feels rewarding.

**MVP Objective:** Ship to the App Store quickly, validate the core loop (generate list → learn → track progress), then iterate with user feedback.

**OKR – First 60 days post-launch:**
- 1000+ downloads (organic + ASO)
- ≥ 40% Day-1 retention
- ≥ 30% Day-7 retention
- ≥ 60% trial start rate (of users who complete onboarding)
- ≥ 20% trial-to-paid conversion
- < 1.5 crash-free rate per user

## Post-MVP Expansion (Not in Scope Now)

- Additional languages (Spanish, German, French…)
- AI-extended lists (auto-suggest related words)
- Community-shared lists
- Spaced repetition with adaptive scheduling (SM-2 algorithm)
- AppsFlyer attribution layer
