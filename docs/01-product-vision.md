# VocabifyX – Product Vision

## Why This App Exists

Most vocabulary learning apps force users into a single language pair (e.g. English → Turkish). VocabifyX is **topic-first**: you pick *what you want to learn about*, not just which languages.

Whether you're preparing for travel, mastering a niche domain (finance, law, medicine), or simply expanding your general vocabulary — VocabifyX generates a personalised keyword list for you via AI, then helps you actually learn and retain it through proven repetition methods.

## Core Value Proposition

> "Tell us what you want to learn & why. We'll give you the exact words you need — and make sure you remember them."

1. **AI-powered list creation** – No manual word hunting. Describe your goal and get a curated keyword list in seconds.
2. **Topic-first approach** – Learn vocabulary around a specific context, not generic word lists.
3. **Retention-first learning** – Flashcards, quizzes, and spaced repetition ensure long-term memory.
4. **Progress you can feel** – XP, levels, streaks, and achievements make every session rewarding.
5. **Learn anything** – A language, a skill domain, travel vocabulary, exam prep — the scope is unlimited.

## Target Audience

| Segment | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Language learners** | Users learning TR ↔ EN or preparing for fluency |
| **Travellers** | People building fast, topic-specific vocab before a trip |
| **Students & Professionals** | Learning domain-specific terminology (law, finance, tech…) |
| **Curious minds** | Anyone who wants to expand their knowledge in a structured way |

## Primary Markets (MVP)

- 🇹🇷 Turkey
- 🇬🇧 English-speaking markets (US, UK, AU)

## Strategic Goal

**Hypothesis:** Users will pay for a focused, AI-powered vocabulary tool that adapts to their specific learning goal, especially when the learning experience is gamified and feels rewarding.

**MVP Objective:** Ship to the App Store quickly, validate the core loop (generate list → learn → track progress), then iterate with user feedback.

**OKR – First 60 days post-launch:**
- 1000+ downloads (organic + ASO)
- ≥ 40% Day-1 retention
- ≥ 30% Day-7 retention
- ≥ 60% trial start rate (of users who complete onboarding)
- ≥ 20% trial-to-paid conversion
- < 1.5 crash-free rate per user

## Post-MVP Expansion (Not in Scope Now)

- Additional languages (Spanish, German, French…)
- AI-extended lists (auto-suggest related words)
- Community-shared lists
- Spaced repetition with adaptive scheduling (SM-2 algorithm)
- AppsFlyer attribution layer
